eTransfer Payment Instructions

Step 1. Log in to your financial institution’s online banking.

Navigate to your interac etransfer services section.

  • you might find it under your pay, transfer, and or move money/funds services area.

Step 2. Add our contact details as a “New Contact”

Please add our company details as a "New Contact" to your eTransfer contacts.

Name:  BDM LTD.

Email:  PAYMENTS@BDMLTD.CA (PLEASE TRIPLE CHECK THE SPELLING!)

Number:  **not required**

Step 3. Security Question and Answer

When prompted to enter a security questions, please enter your current order number.

example: 15001    

When prompted to enter a security answer for your security question, please enter

PAID604   (case sensitive - all capital letters)

 

For everything else, please leave it all blank.

  • Including any notes, invoices or optional fields your bank may have provided.

*Orders are only shipped after your Interac eTransfer has been received*

Once we have received your In eTransfer payment, your order will be processed and shipped within 24-48 hours.

You will automatically receive a confirmation email containing tracking details once your order has been successfully processed, packaged, and shipped.

You must be 19+ to enter this site

Due to the Covid-19, there may be delays in shipment and updates via tracking numbers from Canada Post. Canada Post has currently suspended all guaranteed delivery times during this pandemic.

However, Canada Post is fully operational and packages are still being delivered.

Please contact us via LIVE CHAT or EMAIL us with regards to shipping as there are heavy delays with Canada Post.

When ordering medication, allow extra time for delays of a min. 4 business days.

There are no signature options so all packages requesting signatures will be set to "no" by default. The tracking info will be the only update BUDEM will have.
